# Code of Conduct

Effective Date: 05/28/2025
Maintainer: SantosMods.dev

---

## 1. Purpose

UrbanPlus: World Reimagined is a community-driven mod project maintained by SantosMods.dev.

This Code of Conduct defines expected behavior across all official UrbanPlus spaces, including GitHub, Discord, support channels, documentation, issue discussions, pull requests, forks, mirrors, and affiliated platforms.

The goal is to maintain a respectful, professional, and harassment-free environment for users, contributors, maintainers, and community members.

---

## 2. Scope

This Code applies to all participants involved with UrbanPlus, including:

* Contributors and collaborators
* Users submitting issues, feedback, reports, or suggestions
* Participants in GitHub discussions, pull requests, and issue threads
* Members of official or affiliated Discord spaces
* Users sharing, modifying, mirroring, or redistributing UrbanPlus

All activity connected to the UrbanPlus project is covered by this Code.

---

## 3. Expected Behavior

Participants are expected to:

* Treat others with respect, courtesy, and professionalism.
* Communicate clearly and constructively.
* Use inclusive and appropriate language.
* Respect project maintainers, contributors, and users.
* Follow the project license and attribution requirements.
* Credit SantosMods.dev where required.
* Clearly label unofficial builds, forks, mirrors, or modified versions.
* Report bugs, conflicts, and issues in good faith.
* Resolve disagreements without hostility or personal attacks.

---

## 4. Unacceptable Behavior

The following behavior is not allowed:

* Harassment, abuse, intimidation, or targeted hostility.
* Hate speech, discriminatory remarks, threats, or personal attacks.
* Defamation, doxxing, impersonation, or malicious behavior.
* Spamming, trolling, baiting, or intentionally disruptive conduct.
* Misrepresenting unofficial builds as official SantosMods.dev releases.
* Claiming UrbanPlus or its original work as your own.
* Removing required SantosMods.dev credit.
* Sharing UrbanPlus in violation of its license.
* Selling, reselling, paywalling, or commercially redistributing UrbanPlus without written permission.
* Using UrbanPlus or project spaces for illegal, malicious, or abusive purposes.

---

## 5. Enforcement

Violations may result in one or more of the following actions:

* Warning or formal notice
* Comment, issue, or pull request removal
* Restriction from GitHub participation
* Removal from official Discord or support spaces
* Ban from SantosMods.dev communities
* License enforcement action
* Takedown request or legal action where appropriate

Enforcement decisions are handled by SantosMods.dev maintainers at their discretion.

---

## 6. Reporting

To report a violation or concern, contact SantosMods.dev through:

* Discord: https://santosmods.dev/discord
* Email: [santosmodsdev@gmail.com](mailto:santosmodsdev@gmail.com)

Email is not checked as frequently as Discord. For faster review, use the official Discord when possible.

Reports should include relevant details, links, screenshots, usernames, and context where available.

Reports will be reviewed as privately as reasonably possible and handled according to this Code of Conduct and the UrbanPlus license terms.
