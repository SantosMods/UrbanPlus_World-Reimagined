# UrbanPlus: World Reimagined  
## Developer Contribution Agreement (Binding)

### Plain-English Summary (Read First)
This document explains the rules for contributing to UrbanPlus: World Reimagined.
By contributing, you allow SantosMods.dev to keep, use, modify, or remove your work at any time.
Your work may continue to be used even if you leave, are removed, or stop communicating.
You may only request removal of clearly declared custom assets with 30 days notice.
If you do not agree, do not contribute.

---

## 1. Acceptance & Binding Effect
This Agreement governs all contributions made to **UrbanPlus: World Reimagined**, a project owned and operated by **SantosMods.dev** (“We”, “Us”, “Studio”).

- This Agreement becomes **legally binding upon contribution**.
- No signature is required.
- Continued participation confirms ongoing acceptance.

---

## 2. Definition of Contribution
A “Contribution” includes, but is not limited to:
- Code, scripts, configurations
- Maps, YMAPs, interiors
- Models, textures, audio
- Design concepts, systems, or optimizations

---

## 3. Quality Control (Absolute Discretion)
- We retain the **sole authority** to determine whether a Contribution meets our standards.
- We may reject, modify, or remove any Contribution at any time.
- We are **not required** to use any Contribution.

---

## 4. License Grant (Irrevocable)
By contributing, you grant SantosMods.dev a:

**Perpetual, irrevocable, worldwide, royalty-free, non-exclusive license**  
to use, retain, modify, distribute, sublicense, and integrate your Contribution.

This license:
- Cannot be revoked
- Survives termination, removal, or loss of contact
- Applies even if you are fired, leave, or are removed

---

## 5. Retention After Termination
We may continue using your Contributions even if:
- You are terminated or removed
- You resign or leave voluntarily
- Communication stops
- You request removal

You **waive any right** to force removal, except as defined in Section 6.

---

## 6. Custom Asset Exception (Limited)
Only Contributions that meet **all** of the following qualify:
- Fully custom and originally created by you
- Explicitly declared as “custom assets” **before submission**
- Not based on Studio assets, tools, or references

Removal requests:
- Must be submitted in writing
- Require a **minimum 30-day (1 month) notice**

We retain the right to:
- Keep modified or derivative versions
- Decline removal if the asset is core or integrated

---

## 7. Studio Right to Remove Content
We may remove, replace, or alter any Contribution:
- At any time
- For any reason
- With or without notice

Removal does **not** entitle you to compensation.

---

## 8. No Ownership or Control Claim
- You retain original authorship but **no control** over usage.
- You may not resell, relicense, or reuse Contributions made for this project in competing or derivative projects without written permission.

---

All non-public materials are confidential.

You may not leak, share, or distribute:

Violations may result in:

- Platform or legal enforcement where applicable

- This Agreement does not create employment, partnership, or ownership.
