# UrbanPlus: World Reimagined

**UrbanPlus: World Reimagined** is a comprehensive world transformation project developed by **SantosMods.dev**, focused on redefining the environmental identity of San Andreas through modular, region-based development.

The project launched on **May 22, 2025** and remains under active structured development, with continued focus on map expansion, environmental realism, AI navigation accuracy, and long-term compatibility.

UrbanPlus is designed for clean integration across multiplayer and single-player environments while remaining scalable for future regional expansions.

## Core Capabilities

* **Custom YMAP Development** with reworked and expanded regions
* **Advanced AI Navigation Nodes** improving traffic flow and path logic
* **Environmental Reconstruction** including layout and terrain adjustments
* **Modular Architecture** allowing controlled future expansion
* **Cross-Platform GTA V Support** for FiveM, RageMP, and Story Mode use

## Dependencies

UrbanPlus requires **bob74_ipl v2.4.1** for proper prop handling and world stability.

Due to its MIT license, `bob74_ipl` may be bundled with official UrbanPlus releases to ensure consistent behavior and reduce map conflicts.
